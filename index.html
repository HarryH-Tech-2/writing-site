FAQs

What does "content strategy" mean in practice?

It means I handle the full picture — not just writing. I research your audience, identify keyword opportunities, build editorial calendars, create content briefs, manage production (whether I'm writing or leading a team), and track performance against business goals. Strategy without execution is a slide deck. I do both.

Can you manage a content team, or just produce content yourself?

Both. I've built and managed editorial workflows for teams producing 100+ pieces per month — hiring writers, creating style guides, designing review processes, and running editorial calendars. I can also step in as a solo operator handling the full lifecycle from strategy through execution.

What industries do you specialize in?

My deepest expertise is in fintech, SaaS, and Web3 — I've led content for companies like JP Morgan, BitGo, eToro, and Solana. But the strategic frameworks I use (audience research, keyword strategy, editorial operations) transfer across industries. I pick up new verticals fast.

How do you measure content success?

I tie content to business outcomes, not vanity metrics. That means organic traffic growth, keyword rankings, conversion rates, MQLs generated, and pipeline influenced. I set up tracking from day one and report on what matters to the people who sign off on budgets.
